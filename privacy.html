Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 23 Apr 2026


Welcome to Orbo. Your privacy is a priority for us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide a better experience for all our users.

This falls into three categories:


  • Information You Provide:
    • Account Details: Display name, username, email address and password.
    • Profile Info: Bio, profile picture and profile banner
    • Content: Posts, images, comments and private messages

  • Information Collected Automatically:
    • Usage Data: Posts you like and accounts you follow.
    • Device Info: IP address, browser type and operating system
    • Cookies: Small files used to remember your login and preferences

  • Information from Third Parties:

      If you link other services to you account we may receive information from them.

2. How We Use Your Information

We don’t just collect data for the sake of it.

We use it to:


    1. Operate the Platform: Create your account and display your content.
    2. Personalize Your Feed: Show you posts and ads you’ll actually care about.
    3. Communication: Send you security alerts, updates and marketing.
    4. Safety & Security: Detect and prevent fraud, spam or harassment.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data.

However, we may share information in these scenarios:


  • Public Content: Anything you post publicly is visible to everyone.
  • Service Providers: We work with third parties who help us run the site.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the safety of our users.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or sale of the company.

4. Your Rights and Choices

You are in the driver's seat.

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:


  • Access/Export: Request a copy of the data we have on you.
  • Correction: Fix inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request that we "forget" you and delete your account.
  • Opt-out: Change your settings to limit data collection or personalized ads.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it immediately.
